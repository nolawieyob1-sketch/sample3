About Chernet Degu

The visionary voice behind Cherry Art, shaped by heritage, history, and heart.

Early Life & Passion

Born: 1991
Hometown: Addis Ababa, Ethiopia

Chernet Degu discovered his passion for art at an early age. As a child, he would spend hours sketching the world around him, inspired by the vibrant culture and natural beauty of Addis Ababa. Encouraged by his family, his early creativity blossomed as they provided him with tools to explore his talent.

Artistic Training & Style

In 2011, Chernet enrolled in the Master of Arts and Vocational Training Center in Addis Ababa. He developed his skills in drawing and painting while studying great masters like Leonardo da Vinci. Through tireless practice and self-discovery, he crafted a distinctive visual voice that speaks through every canvas.

“We begin at the end and end at the beginning... No matter how big or small the circle is, it ends the same way for everyone.”

— Chernet Degu

Exhibitions & Achievements

  • 2012 – US Embassy, Addis Ababa
  • 2015 – Atlanta with MAKUSH Art Gallery
  • 2017 – New York Baptist Church
  • 2018 – Hilton Hotel, Addis Ababa
  • 2019 – Golden Tulip Hotel, Addis Ababa
  • 2019 – Sheraton Addis Group Show
  • 2022 – Hilton Hotel with AWIB
  • 2022 – Washington DC with MAKUSH

Beyond the Canvas

Outside the studio, Chernet finds inspiration in nature, Ethiopian culture, and traditional storytelling. When he's not painting, he enjoys playing traditional music and mentoring aspiring young artists in his community — keeping the circle of art alive.
