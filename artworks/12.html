Silent Journey

"Silent Journey" portrays the inward trek of a soul searching for light. It’s not loud, but deeply moving — designed to be felt in silence. The figure feels suspended between moments, between destinations. Layers of muted tone and gentle strokes echo thoughts too heavy for words. This work honors the emotional journeys we take when the world isn’t watching.
