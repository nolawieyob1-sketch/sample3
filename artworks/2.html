Echoes of Time

"Echoes of Time" captures the layers of forgotten memories and unspoken stories passed down through generations. The abstract motion of gold and shadow creates a timeless ripple — as if voices from the past are whispering in the present. This piece invites the viewer to pause and listen, not with ears, but with the soul. Deeply textured and spiritually resonant, it serves as a mirror to ancestral legacy, decay, rebirth, and the undying rhythm of existence.

Medium: Oil on Canvas
Dimensions: 70cm x 100cm
Year: 2024
