Light and Shadow

"Light and Shadow" dances between extremes — brightness and void, clarity and mystery. It's a study in duality, crafted with delicate tension. As light grazes one edge of the figure, darkness holds the other, and in between lies the truth: that beauty is born where the two meet. This piece invites long reflection and quiet realization, like walking through a corridor of thought, where the walls are made of memory and feeling.
